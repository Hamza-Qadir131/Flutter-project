Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our HealthCare app.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

We collect your precise location data (GPS) ONLY when:

  • You activate the emergency/panic button feature
  • Location data is used solely to send your current location to your emergency contacts
  • Location data is not stored permanently and is only used during the emergency alert process

2. Contact Information

We collect and store:

  • Emergency contact phone numbers that you provide
  • This information is stored locally on your device
  • Used only for sending emergency SMS alerts

3. Health and Fitness Data

We collect and store:

  • Step count data using device sensors
  • Water intake tracking data
  • Sleep tracking information
  • All health data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers

Required Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Location Permissions: Required for emergency features to send your location to contacts
  • SMS Permission: Required to send emergency messages to your contacts
  • Activity Recognition: Required for step counting feature
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback and alerts

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for:

  • Providing emergency assistance through location sharing and SMS alerts
  • Tracking your fitness and health goals
  • Improving your app experience

Data Storage and Security

All your personal data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to external servers
  • Not shared with third parties
  • Protected using Android's built-in security features

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Modify or remove emergency contacts at any time
  • Disable any feature by revoking respective permissions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
